Simple Pursuits

Simple Pursuits is a little shop based in Iowa that loves the big impact of small changes in our lives. We're junkies for minimalism and less complicated lives. Common sense, practicality, and simplicity rule the day.

In the midst of those daily decisions that go a little counterculture, it can be hard to stay motivated. So we started this poster line to encourage us and others along the way.

All of our posters are screen printed by hand on high-quality printmaking paper. Posters come in two sizes - 8x10 and 15x20 - and a variety of ink colors.
